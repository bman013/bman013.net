About us

Foursight is a boutique security consultancy service based in Brisbane, Australia. We aim to mature the security posture of Australian businesses, while educating and and raising awareness to our clients of the modern security landscape.

We are not just individual professionals, but a team working towards changing the way to secure your data and technology stack. To us, this is not just a job- this is our passion. We pride ourselves on bringing the most innovative and quality-driven outcomes to our clients.

Our services

Society is consistently being exposed to news of organisations suffering devistating impacts as a result of Cyber Security incidents. Because of this, Australian business is being placed under higher security pressure from their clients (current and potential), insurers and financiers, as well as regulatory bodies, to show progress and compliance with purpose built industry security standards.

Foursight offer a range of Cyber Security services to help organisations better protect their assets and data. These services range from general consultancy and advice, through to niche technical expertise from penetration testing through to security incident response. See below for a full list of services offered.

Security Assessments

Foursight provide best of breed black, grey or white penetration testing services across a range of platforms or environments. Our CREST Certified Testing team will work closely to ensure accurate scoping and quality overall delivery.

Red & Purple Teaming

We venture beyond standard penetration testing services to provide full attack simulation, targetting agreed scenarios within your organisation. Augment this service with our purple team for security control validation and defensive security uplift.

Incident Response

Foursight will work closely with your business provide expert assistance through any phase of your response activities. This includes technical responders, incident and communication management, as well as general advice and guidance.
